PROGRAMS · DEVELOPMENT

The first years are the foundation.

Cognitive and motor development for ages 3 to 7, the foundation for every program that follows.

Development

Four pillars of every session

Every session at BrightPath is built around four quiet ideas. They are not boxes to tick, they are the texture of the day.

01

Curiosity

Asking, watching, wondering. The starting point of every lesson.

02

Movement

Fine and gross motor work woven into the day, threading, cutting, climbing, balancing, without drills or pressure.

03

Expression

Drawing, building, telling stories. Many ways to be understood.

04

Together

Listening, taking turns, repairing a small disagreement. The skills of being with others.

The method

A predictable shape, every day.

Step 1

Welcome circle

A calm beginning. Names, song, today's plan. Children settle into the room.

Step 2

Focused work

A small project, a story, a puzzle. Quiet hands and quiet minds.

Step 3

Active play

Movement, music, the body remembering. Outside when the weather lets us.

Step 4

Reflection

What did we make today? What did we notice? A moment together before we go home.

Age tracks

Three rooms, three rhythms.

3-4 First steps

Two short mornings a week. Small group, lots of song, lots of movement, the gentlest first taste of structured time.

4-5 Wider world

Three mornings. Stories, numbers in play, big art, group games. The year children begin to see themselves as learners.

6-7 Ready hands

Four mornings. Reading and writing emerge alongside building, drawing, and talking through ideas, on the way to school.
