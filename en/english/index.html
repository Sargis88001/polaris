What your child will learn

A language they can actually use.

Each level builds on the last — speaking, listening, reading, and writing developed together so no skill outpaces another.

🗣️

Confident spoken English

Children speak from lesson one. Every session is mostly conversation — real situations, games, and guided discussion where mistakes are expected and welcome.

📖

Reading & listening comprehension

Books, songs, short films, and news-adapted texts. Children learn to extract meaning, infer context, and engage with authentic English from the earliest levels.

📐

Grammar in context

Grammar is introduced through use, not memorisation. Children encounter a structure many times in real speech before they are asked to name or explain it.

✍️

Writing & vocabulary

From simple sentences to structured paragraphs. Vocabulary is built through spaced repetition and context rather than word lists, so it stays.

FAQ

Common questions

How do you decide which level group my child joins?

We run a short placement session — about twenty minutes, with a written component and a short spoken conversation. It is relaxed, not a test. We use it to find the group where your child will be challenged but never lost.

Does my child need any English before starting?

No. Our Starters group begins from zero — no prior exposure assumed. Children who already know some English are placed in Elementary or higher based on the placement session.

How quickly can a child move up a level?

Based on readiness, not the calendar. Some children move up mid-year, others take two full years at a level. We discuss progress with parents at the end of each term and flag any proposed changes before making them.

Is there an exam at the end of the year?

Optional mock Cambridge exams (YLE / KET / PET) are available for older groups. They are never required. We write a short termly report for every child regardless, which describes what they can now do in concrete, specific terms.
