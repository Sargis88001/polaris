PROGRAMS · ENGLISH

English that comes out of your mouth, not just your textbook.

Communicative English from first words to fluency. Speaking-first lessons with grammar woven through.

English

Our approach

Children speak from the first lesson. We build from real situations, real songs, and real conversations, then weave grammar through the year so it never feels like a wall to climb.

01

Speaking first

Confidence comes from use. Most of every lesson is talking, listening, and trying. Mistakes are welcome.

02

Real materials

Books, songs, films, news. Language is a window onto the world, not a textbook chapter.

03

Small groups

Eight to twelve children. Every voice gets airtime in every lesson.

04

Honest progress

A short termly report, written like a letter, that tells you exactly what your child can now do.
