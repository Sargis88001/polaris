FAQ

Common questions

What age can children start?

From age 4, with no prior experience needed. Our Beginners group is specifically designed for children who have never been on a mat before. We place every new child after a short, informal assessment.

What should my child wear?

Comfortable, fitted sportswear with no zips, hard buttons, or loose strings. Bare feet on the mat. Long hair should be tied back. We provide all equipment.

How often should my child attend?

Two sessions per week is the minimum for meaningful physical progress. Children in the Junior team attend three times. One session a week is fine for children who come primarily for enjoyment and general fitness.

Is there a performance or competition at the end of term?

Advanced and Junior team groups participate in regional competitions when children are ready and willing. It is never required. All groups hold an informal showcase at the end of the year, which is low-key and celebratory rather than competitive.
