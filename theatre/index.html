PROGRAMS · THEATRE

Voice, presence, and the joy of being heard.

A theatre program that turns shy whispers into clear, confident voices, ending every term in a real performance.

Theatre

What a term looks like

Each term ends in a real performance, with lights, an audience, and a curtain call. The journey there is twelve quiet weeks of voice, presence, and trust.

Weeks 1-3

Voice and breath

Warm-ups, games, listening exercises. The room becomes safe enough to be heard in.

Weeks 4-7

Scene work

Short scenes, simple texts, real stakes. Children take turns leading and listening.

Weeks 8-10

Building the show

The piece comes together. Lines learned, blocking set, costumes chosen.

Weeks 11-12

Performance

Two evenings, one matinee, a full audience. Then a quiet conversation about what we learned.

Auditions are open.

No experience needed, just a child who would like to try. Send us a message and we will book a workshop.
